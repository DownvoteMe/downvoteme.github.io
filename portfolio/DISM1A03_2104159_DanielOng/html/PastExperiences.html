My life before Singapore Polytechnic.

Life in High school

Before Singapore Polytechnic, I found school life challenging because I had to juggle school subjects and my own hobbies - game development and art design. I had little sleep as scoring decent grades meant that I needed to sacrifice time for sleep in order to study for my subjects. However, I did learn a lot from the whole experience, and I am grateful for my teachers who have supported me through the way.

Primary School

I enrolled into Maris Stella High School when I was 5 years old. Primary school was the foundation to my education. I did well in class, but I had no clear goal in life yet. Over the years, however, I did grow complacent, causing me to take my studies lightly. After scoring 242 for my PSLE, I enrolled into Maris Stella High School again for my secondary school life.

Secondary School

Secondary life was when I realized that the subjects were much harder than before, causing me to rethink my complacency. I started working on building talents for my working life, while studying to catch up on the subjects I could not do well in. The problem was that it was impossible to do both at the same time, forcing me to work overnight. Looking back at my past, I am glad that I managed to go through the challenges I faced without giving up.
